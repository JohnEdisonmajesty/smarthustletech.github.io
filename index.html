About Us

A new revolution of advanced technology development in Africa covering the technological sphere with services and training in 15+ tech (IT) fields.

The idea for SMART HUSTLE TECH was inspired by the question, "How many people will ever be successful if success was exclusively dependent on hard work?" Given that success in life does not depend on hard work or physical strength, Smart Hustle Tech has recognized a challenge and developed a platform where people will have the chance to learn and pursue careers in IT in +15 different fields. Hiring businesses are looking for individuals with tech knowledge and skills and newly formed companies can’t skyrocket or even survive without leveraging massively on technology. More than most other firms and with special services for both large and small businesses, Smart Hustle Tech offers tech training to its clients. We welcome clients at all levels who are enthusiastic and seeking to learn new skills in IT to attend our training.
